Building

Parking

Parking Lot #1 on Library Road is few meters before the building. Parking Lot #2 is behind the building on Campus Avenue. R4 is right after the building (available for visitors after 5pm). There are other parking lots across the Carleton University campus.

Parking can be paid using either the pay and display machines or the text-to-park service iParked.ca. The [parking evening rate is $8.00] (https://carleton.ca/parking/visitors/parking-rates/)
