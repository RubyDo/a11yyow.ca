Impacts of Bill C-81
by Max Brault

Are you ready for is about to happen?

Max Brault is a bilingual Federal legislation and policy leader who leverages 19+ years of progressive public sector experience focusing on employment equity and the advancement of workplace accessibility and career development for people with disabilities. Guides federal government toward achieving mission, vision, and goals of new and progressive accessibility legislation. Provides strategic action plans and builds consensus on initiatives as a forward-thinking government liaison. As Senior Advisor, recently completed 2 year engagement exercise with Deputy Ministers and broad range of other internal stakeholders, resulting in a successful proposal for the Federal Employees with Disabilities task force. This provided the Clerk with vital resources and enabled them to enact Accessibility Legislation internally among public sector workplaces. Seeking now to harness key insights gained during that proposal development to lead and advise federal government on overcoming barriers in procurement, employment, career development, and technology that will help successfully realize legislative objectives.
